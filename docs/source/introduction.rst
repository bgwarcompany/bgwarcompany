Introduction 
============

Barony of Bryn Gwlad
--------------------
The Barony of Bryn Gwlad is the Austin, Texas chapter of the Society for Creative Anachronism

War Company
-----------
The Bryn Gwlad War Company is a the combat unit for our area.  
The War Company is open to join and participates in all SCA combat (both chivalric and rapier).
